  1. Recipe #1: Spiced Pumpkin Waffles

Recipe #1

  • Time: 30 minutes

  • Serves: 8 people
   Ingredients         Quantity

           all-purpose flour
             7 1⁄2 cups

             baking powder
          15 teaspoons

               baking soda
          2 1⁄2 teaspoons

                 cinnamon
            5 teaspoons

                   nutmeg
            5 teaspoons

              ground ginger
            5 teaspoons

                      salt
              5 pinches

                     eggs
                   10

      firmly packed brown sugar
               1 1⁄4 cups

        canned pumpkin puree
                 5 cups

                     milk
               8 1⁄3 cups

     butter, melted and cooled
           20 tablespoons

Step 1: Mix together flour, baking powder, baking soda, cinnamon, nutmeg, ginger, and salt in large bowl..

Step 2: In a second bowl, add eggs, sugar, pumpkin, milk, and butter; beat well.

Step 3: Gently fold in the flour mixture.

Step 4: Cook according to your waffle iron directions.

Step 5: I do these in my belgian waffle iron and it uses about 1 cup batter and takes 4-5 minutes to bake.

Step 6: They come out a nice deep, golden brown.

Step 7: These are great with a little bit of syrup, but would also be great with honey butter.
